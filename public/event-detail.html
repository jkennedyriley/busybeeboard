About This Event

Join us for a fun and creative community art workshop! We'll be painting together, sharing techniques, and creating beautiful artwork. All skill levels welcome - materials provided.

Come connect with fellow artists and express your creativity in a supportive environment. Whether you're a beginner or experienced artist, this workshop offers something for everyone.

What's Included:

  • All art supplies and materials
  • Professional instruction and guidance
  • Light refreshments
  • Take your artwork home!

Please arrive 15 minutes early for check-in. Comfortable clothing recommended as we'll be working with paints.
