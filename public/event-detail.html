About This Event

Join us for a fun and creative community art workshop! We'll be painting together, sharing techniques, and creating beautiful artwork. All skill levels welcome - materials provided.

Come connect with fellow artists and express your creativity in a supportive environment. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced artist, you'll find something to inspire you.

What's included:

  • All painting materials and supplies
  • Step-by-step guidance from local artists
  • Light refreshments
  • Take your artwork home!
