About This Event

Join us for a fun and creative community art workshop! We'll be painting together, sharing techniques, and creating beautiful artwork that celebrates our neighborhood.

This workshop is perfect for artists of all skill levels - whether you're a complete beginner or have been painting for years. Our experienced facilitators will guide you through different techniques and help you create something truly special.

What's included:

  • All art supplies and materials
  • Canvas or paper for your artwork
  • Light refreshments and coffee
  • Take home your finished piece

What to bring:

  • Just yourself and your creativity!
  • Comfortable clothes (we might get a little messy)
  • A friend if you'd like - it's more fun together

This event is part of our ongoing community engagement series, designed to bring neighbors together through shared creative experiences. We believe art has the power to connect people and build stronger communities.
