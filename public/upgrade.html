❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ↓
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
What happens if I exceed my event limit? ↓
If you reach your monthly event limit, you'll need to wait until the next billing cycle or upgrade to a higher plan to post more events.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits? ↓
Yes! We offer special pricing for verified non-profit organizations. Contact our support team with your non-profit documentation for details.
Can I change plans later? ↓
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.
What payment methods do you accept? ↓
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) and debit cards. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
