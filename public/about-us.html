About BusyBee Board

We're on a mission to reconnect communities through local events, independent of social media algorithms and traditional media bias.

üéØ Our Mission

In many communities, finding local events has become surprisingly difficult. Social media platforms prioritize corporate and influencer content, while local news sources have shrunk dramatically, leaving a gap in visibility for grassroots events.

BusyBee Board fills this gap by providing a dedicated platform where anyone can discover and promote local events, ensuring that smaller gatherings and local initiatives get the visibility they deserve.

Free for Everyone, Always
We believe community connection shouldn't cost anything

‚ú® What Makes Us Different

Community-First Approach: Unlike social media platforms that bury small events under corporate content, BusyBee Board prioritizes local, community-driven listings. It's a return to grassroots connections, putting people and real-world events at the center.

No Algorithm Bias: Your events are shown based on relevance to location and user preferences, not paid promotion or engagement metrics. Every event gets a fair chance to be discovered.

Local Focus: We prioritize events by geographic proximity, helping you discover what's happening right in your neighborhood.

Transparent Monetization: When organizers choose to promote their events, it's clearly marked. Our revenue comes from optional paid promotions, not hidden algorithms or data selling.

üîí Technology & Privacy

Privacy-First Design: We collect only the information necessary to provide our service. We don't sell user data, and we're transparent about what information we gather and how it's used.

Modern, Reliable Platform: Built with cutting-edge web technologies, our platform is fast, secure, and accessible on all devices.

Spam Protection: We use CAPTCHA and email verification to keep the platform clean and trustworthy for everyone.

Accessibility: We're committed to making BusyBee Board accessible to everyone, following WCAG standards for inclusive design.
